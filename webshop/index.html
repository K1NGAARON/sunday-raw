Key features

Full customisation to match your brand

Every shop is uniquely designed to reflect your brand identity. From logos and color schemes to layout and navigation, your webshop will look and feel like an extension of your brand. Whether it’s sleek and modern or fun and playful, we ensure your merchandise shines.

Sell with virtual coins or real money

Our shops are versatile: choose to sell your merchandise with real currency or integrate virtual coins and points systems for employee rewards or loyalty programs. Create a seamless shopping experience for your team or customers, no matter the payment method.

Centralized management of your merchandise

Our platform simplifies the process of managing your products, inventory, and orders. Track stock in real time, manage different product categories, and integrate with fulfillment centers to ship globally—all from one place

Data-driven insights for smart decision-making

Track your shop’s performance with detailed analytics and reporting tools. Understand which products are best-sellers, measure the impact of your promotions, and optimize inventory management to reduce costs and increase profitability.

Scalable solutions for any size business

Whether you’re a small business launching your first product or a global enterprise managing a vast inventory, our platform is built to scale with you. Easily add new products, manage large-scale orders, and customize features as your business grows.

The full suite of e-commerce marketing tools

Seamlessly manage all your shops from one platform

Our Sunday platform simplifies the management of your customer, employee, and partner merchandise shops, allowing you to oversee all your e-commerce needs in one place. Whether you’re running multiple programs or targeting different audiences, our platform provides tailored functionality for each shop to ensure streamlined operations and success.

With Sunday, you can assign stock, users, and reward coins per individual shop, giving you complete control and flexibility. It’s the perfect solution for businesses looking to manage distinct programs while maintaining an organised and efficient workflow.
