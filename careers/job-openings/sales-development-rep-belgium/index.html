We are looking for a

Sales development representative

As a Sales Development Representative you join the international SDR team and pair up with Account Executives. You work closely together to develop new opportunities in the market.

Location: Roeselare (HQ)
Department: Sales

Job description

We are looking for an entrepreneurial
& enthusiastic Sales Development Representative:

  • You manage and qualify inbound leads.
  • You make proactive contact with potential B2B clients by using LinkedIn, networking spaces, and so on. The more personal and creative your approach, the better.
  • You introduce your new contacts to your sales colleagues and plan the next sales meetings with them.
  • You’re a true social media ambassador and would love to expand your professional network.

Our offer

  • You’ll work at our brand new HQ in Roeselare.
  • You’ll get 1:1 sales training & coaching in our intense onboarding program.
  • You get in touch with several corporate B2B clients like Duvel, NMBS, IJsboerke, Crocky,... .
  • Hard work pays off: we work with a transparent commission structure.
  • You’ll work with 60 passionate colleagues across Europe.
  • In our team you make the difference. Flat hierarchy & short communication lines are key!
  • Growth is one of our key values. Thanks to our customized development plan, every individual gets the chance to grow.
  • We always end the week with a Friday afterwork.
  • Cool Sunday swag for you to wear proudly!

Your skills

  • You’re interested in getting to know new people and listening to their needs.
  • You know your way around LinkedIn (or you want to learn it) and you like to share new contents.
  • You like to think outside the box and use your creativity to contact new clients.
  • You get energy when you reach your targets.
  • You’re fluent in both Dutch and English.
  • Experience with HubSpot (sales crm software) is a plus.
