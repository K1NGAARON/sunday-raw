Sales development representative Germany

Location: Berlin
Department: Sales

Sunday is a dynamic scale-up located in five different countries across Europe.

Eight years ago we started in the small living room of one of our CEO’s and today it has become an organization with more than 50 highly-motivated & talented individuals that are now designing and creating company fashion for brands like Zalando, TikTok, Google, Hubspot, Gorillas, …

Our business can be seen as an HR, Marketing & Sales solution that increases a company’s brand awareness. We are not just selling products, we are helping our clients transform their employees and clients into real ambassadors through company fashion.

Want to be part of this story?

Job description

We are looking for an entrepreneurial & enthusiastic Sales Development Representative:

As a Sales Development Representative you join the international SDR team and pair up with an Account Executive. You work closely together to develop new opportunities in the market.

  • You manage and qualify inbound leads.
  • You make proactive contact with potential B2B clients by using LinkedIn, networking spaces, and so on. The more personal and creative your approach, the better.
  • You introduce your new contacts to your sales colleague and plan the next sales meetings with them.
  • You’re a true social media ambassador and would love to expand your professional network.

Your skills

  • You’re interested in getting to know new people and listening to their needs.
  • You know your way around LinkedIn (or you want to learn it) and you like to share new contents.
  • You like to think outside the box and use your creativity to contact new clients.
  • You get energy when you reach your targets.
  • You’re fluent in both Dutch and English.
  • Experience with HubSpot (sales crm software) is a plus.

Our offer

  • You’ll be part of an international fast growing company that creates cool company fashion.
  • You’ll get 1:1 sales training & coaching by our sales development manager Birgit in an intense onboarding program.
  • You get the chance to grow on a personal and professional level within Sunday, internally and internationally.
  • You will expand your professional network and get in touch with several corporate B2B clients like Spotify, TikTok, Zalando, Netflix, Mastercard, Gorillas, …
  • You’ll get two Sunday collections a year for you to wear proudly!
