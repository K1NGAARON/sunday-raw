Making a great impact with this fast growing IT scale-up

Das Briefing

In terms of design requirements, the Lansweeper team was very clear: they wanted a unisex collection where, above all, the design had to be very simple and wearable. Their orange fire color forms a fun and eye-catching accent in the clothing, while the other colors are neutral black and gray.
The t-shirt got some extra spice by giving the breast pocket an extra motif: the company name is repeated here in a subtle way. In addition, the sweaters received an extra running label, a small but nice touch that still ensures that the sweaters are clearly customized for Lansweeper.
