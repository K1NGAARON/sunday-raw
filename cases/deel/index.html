The most efficient merch strategy in event management

Challenges for the customer

How can we make our merchandise approach for events easier and more efficient?

Sunday's solution

By using the Sunday Wardrobe, all merchandise is centralised in one place. Warehousing, fulfillment and shipments are taken over by Sunday.

Industry: HRMS
Company size: +5000 employees
Topic: How to streamline global event merchandise

"Event management is the art of turning chaos into a masterpiece, where every detail matters. While stress may knock on the door, it's our expertise that ensures it never gets a seat at the table."

A quote that is recognisable for the company Deel. About the company: Deel helps thousands of companies expand globally with unmatched speed and flexibility. It's global hiring, HR and payroll in just one system.

More than 400 events in 65+ countries

Each year Deel organises/takes part in about 400 events: teambuilding events, fair booths, networking events, … Organising that amount of events on a global scale asks for some very skilled project management.

The goal of their events are mainly to create brand awareness in different countries or for HR purposes (team buildings, finding new talent, …). Part of their event strategy is the use of premium, personalised merchandise. Despite being fully convinced of the strength of merchandise, deploying it was still a hassle. This all changed when they were introduced to the Sunday Wardrobe.

“One of the most significant challenges we faced was dealing with numerous local suppliers for our merchandise. With events happening globally, each supplier spoke different languages and had varying quality standards, making follow-ups a constant headache. This all changed when we discovered Sunday and their incredible wardrobe solution.” - Triana, DEEL

Centralising all merchandise

Deel's belief in the power of merchandise is evident in their approach. “Sunday helped us understand its impact even better; they have centralized it all for us in what we fondly call the 'Sunday Wardrobe.'” Having all their merchandise in one place has been a game-changer. They no longer have to juggle between different suppliers worldwide. Sunday takes care of it all.

Sunday’s centralized system means companies no longer need to worry about language barriers, quality discrepancies, or late deliveries. “Sunday has streamlined our process, handling everything from warehousing to shipping. This has not only saved us valuable time and effort but has also significantly reduced the stress that comes with organizing events on such a large scale.”

Consistency in brand and quality

Moreover, Deel’s approach of using merchandise not only for their team but also as impactful giveaways to prospects and clients has strengthened their brand presence. The company understands the importance of consistency and quality, ensuring that every piece of merchandise reflects their values.

In the world of event management, where precision and reliability are non-negotiable, Sunday has proven to be an invaluable partner when it comes to the design and use of merchandise. With our expertise, dedication, and innovative approach, we aim to transform companies' merchandise strategy into a clear, easy and successful experience!
