Solidarity, connectivity and a sense of community: this is what merch does for Techwolf

Using merchandise as a tool to boost team feeling can create a sense of unity and belonging among team members, fostering a shared identity and pride in being part of the group. Team jerseys or customized merchandise not only enhances team spirit but also acts as a visible symbol of camaraderie, strengthening the bond and motivation within the team.

Challenges for the customer

Their team is very important and they want to do everything in their power to boost the community feeling within the team. Branded clothing is a great way to do this but how can we create premium designs of high quality?

Sunday's solution

Integrating company fashion for special occasions and for welcoming new hires, so every employee feels part of the team. The Sunday design experts create the best fitted designs and guide companies through the entire design process.
