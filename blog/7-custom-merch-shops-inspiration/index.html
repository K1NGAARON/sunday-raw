7 Custom merchandise webshops you’ll want for your brand

In the fast-evolving world of corporate branding and engagement, custom merchandise is an essential tool for connecting with both employees and customers. At Sunday, we specialize in creating custom merchandise webshops that enable brands to effortlessly distribute and sell their merchandise in a professional and efficient way. Whether your goal is to offer employee rewards, boost brand visibility, or sell exclusive, limited-edition items, our webshops provide the perfect solution. Let’s dive into how some of our clients are using these webshops to take their brand to the next level.

Bitpanda Boutique: Seamless shopping with digital coins

The Bitpanda Boutique is a perfect example of how to combine branding with an innovative shopping experience. Offering a sleek and modern interface, the boutique features an exclusive range of branded merchandise, including premium T-shirts, singlets, and socks. But what truly sets this webshop apart is its integration of Panda Coins—Bitpanda’s internal digital currency. Employees and customers alike can use these coins to buy merchandise, making it a seamless experience for those already engaged with the brand’s ecosystem. The emphasis on sustainability also adds a meaningful layer, as every 50 orders contribute to tree planting, further aligning the merchandise with Bitpanda’s eco-conscious values.

This webshop showcases how easy it can be to offer rewards in a branded, exclusive way. The thoughtful use of digital currency not only encourages interaction within the brand’s ecosystem but also gives employees and customers a convenient, cashless way to get their hands on stylish, sustainable Bitpanda gear. The mix of modern design, usability, and an eco-friendly message makes this boutique stand out as a high-end shopping destination for Bitpanda fans and team members.

Human8 brand fashion: Merging sustainability with corporate identity

For Human8, their custom webshop is much more than a simple merchandise store—it’s a reflection of their values. The Human8 shop features a collection of wearables crafted from sustainable materials, ensuring that each product aligns with the company’s eco-conscious and diversity-driven philosophy. From bright and stylish socks to tote bags and sports jerseys, the range not only promotes the brand but also underscores its commitment to social responsibility. Employees are invited to represent Human8 with pride, knowing that the products they receive are produced with care for both people and the planet.

The user experience is designed to make ordering straightforward and enjoyable, with the entire range available for employees to browse and redeem using the company’s branded currency. The shop also allows Human8 to maintain a strong connection with their global team, no matter where they’re located. This isn’t just about distributing merchandise; it’s about strengthening brand unity and celebrating the company’s diverse workforce through products that resonate with their core values.

Interstellar collection: Bold, minimalist merch with a futuristic edge

The Interstellar Collection takes merchandise to a whole new level with its futuristic, sleek webshop. Focused on a minimalistic aesthetic, the shop offers everything from body warmers to hoodies, all in a sharp black and white color palette that reflects the brand’s modern, cutting-edge identity. Customers can purchase items using “Stars,” a branded currency that adds a playful yet exclusive touch to the shopping experience. This store is all about creating a streamlined and straightforward buying process, making it easy for employees or customers to get their hands on limited-edition, premium Interstellar gear.

What makes this webshop particularly engaging is its focus on exclusivity. By incorporating a points system, Interstellar not only encourages ongoing engagement with the brand but also rewards loyalty in a meaningful way. The overall design speaks to the brand’s innovation, offering a shop that feels as futuristic as the products it sells, with a shopping experience that’s as smooth as the sleek design of their merchandise.

Deel x Sunday varsity jacket: Limited-Edition exclusivity done right

When it comes to building buzz around exclusive merchandise, the Deel x Sunday varsity jacket webshop sets a high standard. This platform was developed as part of a limited-edition campaign, rewarding those who engage in meetings with a stylish, high-quality varsity jacket. The process is simple but effective: after attending a qualified meeting, users receive a unique coupon code that allows them to claim their jacket through the shop. It’s a fantastic example of how a custom merchandise webshop can be used to incentivize business engagement and build a sense of exclusivity around a coveted product.

The design is clean and straightforward, focusing all attention on the jacket itself—an item that carries with it a sense of prestige for those who earn it. The carefully designed campaign drives user participation while fostering a stronger relationship between Deel and its clients. With the added FAQ section to answer all potential questions, the user experience is seamless from start to finish, ensuring that customers can easily get their hands on this limited-time offering.

Chiliz swag shop: High-energy merch for brand ambassadors

For fans of Chiliz, the Chiliz swag shop is the go-to destination for vibrant and stylish brand gear. Whether it’s eye-catching gift boxes, sleek hoodies, or other casual wear, this shop perfectly embodies the energetic, fan-driven nature of the brand. The user-friendly interface allows shoppers to easily browse and purchase items that represent the bold, colorful identity of Chiliz. The shop reflects the brand’s commitment to making fans feel like part of a larger community, all while sporting casual, stylish gear that suits a wide range of tastes.

With a bright, dynamic design that reflects the brand’s lively personality, the Chiliz Swag Shop is all about making merchandise accessible and fun. Fans can engage with the brand in a tangible way, while the smooth purchase experience ensures that buying is as exciting as the products themselves. Whether fans are grabbing a gift box for a friend or adding a new hoodie to their collection, this shop delivers both style and substance in equal measure.

Proximus collection: Playful merch for proud employees

The Proximus collection shop offers a colorful, fun, and engaging range of branded merchandise, from socks to sliders, designed specifically for employees. The design of the shop reflects the playful, modern identity of Proximus, offering a wide variety of items that allow employees to show off their brand pride in style. The shop is designed for ease of use, allowing employees to redeem items quickly and effortlessly, creating a smooth, engaging shopping experience that also doubles as a unique reward system.

What sets the Proximus Collection apart is the thoughtfulness behind the product design, with each item carefully crafted to align with the company’s vibrant brand identity. Employees aren’t just getting branded merch—they’re receiving high-quality products that they’re excited to wear and use, further building a sense of unity within the team. The playful, yet professional design of the webshop enhances this experience, making it easy and enjoyable for employees to explore the full range of offerings.

iO Goodieworld: Exclusive merchandise with a personal touch

At iO, merchandise is all about exclusivity and engagement. Designed for employees and customers who receive special coupon codes, this webshop makes purchasing high-quality branded gear an exciting experience. From backpacks to water bottles and hoodies, the items are designed to be functional yet stylish, with limited-edition offerings that make each product feel unique and special. The elegant design of the webshop mirrors the premium nature of the products themselves, ensuring that the shopping experience is as polished as the brand.

What makes iO Goodieworld particularly appealing is how it creates a personal connection through the use of coupon codes. The store feels exclusive and accessible only to those who are in the know, which enhances the sense of value around each item. The sleek layout and ease of navigation make it simple for users to browse and purchase, ensuring that the experience is smooth from start to finish, whether they’re buying a backpack or a cozy, limited-edition hoodie.
