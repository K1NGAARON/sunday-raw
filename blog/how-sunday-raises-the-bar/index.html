From strategy to delivery: How Sunday raises the bar beyond traditional suppliers

In today's competitive landscape, standing out is more challenging than ever, especially for companies that operate on a global scale. Merchandise isn't just about putting a logo on a product; it's about creating a tangible extension of your brand that resonates deeply with your audience—whether they're employees, partners, or customers.

At Sunday, we've redefined what merchandise can do for your brand by offering a comprehensive, end-to-end service that goes far beyond what traditional suppliers provide.

1. A mission that puts your Brand first

At Sunday, our mission is both simple and profound: to create merchandise that people love to wear, not just any day, but on a Sunday—a day synonymous with relaxation, comfort, and personal expression. This philosophy is at the heart of everything we do because we believe that when people proudly wear your merchandise, they're not just wearing clothes—they're becoming ambassadors for your brand.

Merchandise is one of the most powerful marketing channels available, but it's also one of the most complex. Our goal at Sunday is to simplify this complexity for you, much like how MailChimp revolutionized email marketing. We aim to make merchandise marketing just as intuitive and effective by creating high-quality, beautifully designed items that can be shipped globally with just a few clicks. Our approach ensures that your merchandise isn't just delivered—it's delivered with impact, and you can measure that impact every step of the way.

2. Tailored to the needs of growing companies

As companies grow, so do the complexities involved in managing their brand. For larger organizations with over 250 employees, particularly those with an international focus, merchandise isn't just a nice-to-have; it's a critical tool for brand building, employee engagement, and customer loyalty. However, managing merchandise at this scale introduces new challenges—challenges that Sunday is uniquely equipped to handle.

For smaller companies, ordering merchandise might be as simple as placing an order for 20 t-shirts and handing them out at the office. But for larger organizations, the stakes are much higher. Not everyone is in the office, your brand needs to be represented consistently across different regions, and your merchandise strategy must align with broader company goals.

At Sunday, we specialize in working with companies that require a high level of customization, quality, and strategic alignment. We understand that your merchandise isn't just about the items themselves; it's about ensuring that every piece is perfectly on-brand, whether it's a hoodie for your employees in Berlin or a promotional gift for clients in New York. Our comprehensive approach ensures that your brand is represented consistently, effectively, and beautifully, no matter where in the world your merchandise is used.

3. Deep customization and pixel-perfect personalization

Customization is at the core of what we do at Sunday. We believe that your merchandise should be as unique as your brand, which is why we go to great lengths to ensure that every detail is perfect. Whether it's matching your exact Pantone color, creating custom designs that reflect your brand's identity, or ensuring that the packaging is just as on-brand as the product itself, we take customization to the next level.

Our design team, which includes skilled fashion designers, uses advanced 3D software to create precise models of your products before they go into production. This technology allows us to show you exactly how your merchandise will look, right down to the smallest details. No more guessing, no more imagining—just a clear, accurate representation of the final product. This level of precision is especially important when multiple stakeholders need to approve a design. With Sunday, everyone can see exactly what they're getting, ensuring that the final product is pixel-perfect and ready to make an impact.

4. End-to-End service that simplifies the complex

Traditional merchandise suppliers often leave you with a box of t-shirts at your doorstep and little else. But at Sunday, we handle the entire process from start to finish, ensuring that every aspect of your merchandise strategy is aligned with your brand's goals and executed flawlessly.

Our end-to-end service begins with consulting. We start by understanding your company's objectives, whether it's boosting employee engagement, strengthening customer loyalty, or expanding brand awareness. We then break these objectives down into actionable tracks, which we call Team Empowerment, Partner Engagement, and Fanbase Establishment. Each track is designed to address specific goals, both short-term and long-term, and we work with you to develop targeted campaigns that deliver measurable results.

For example, if your goal is to improve employee retention, we might design an onboarding kit as part of the Team Empowerment track. This isn't just about choosing products; it's about crafting an experience that makes new employees feel welcome, valued, and excited to be part of your team. We handle everything from product selection and design to production, distribution, and feedback collection. Our MerchMetrics engine, which is integrated into our Sunday Wardrobe platform, allows us to track the impact of your merchandise in real-time, providing you with valuable insights that can be used to refine future campaigns.

5. Unmatched quality and consistency

When it comes to merchandise, consistency is key. Traditional suppliers often rely on pre-made blanks, which can lead to inconsistencies in sizes, colors, and availability over time. This can be particularly problematic for companies that require high levels of quality and consistency in their branding.

At Sunday, we've solved this problem by creating our own patterns in-house. This means that every item you order from us is made to your exact specifications, ensuring that your merchandise is consistent not just from one order to the next, but over years. Whether you order a batch of t-shirts today or a batch of hoodies next year, you can be confident that the sizes, colors, and quality will be identical.

This consistency extends to sizing as well. Once your employees have their sizes in our system, they can order any product, from t-shirts to sweaters, with the assurance that it will fit perfectly. This level of precision and reliability is unmatched in the industry and provides peace of mind for companies that take their brand seriously.

6. Global reach with seamless logistics

Managing global logistics for merchandise can be a daunting task, especially when you're dealing with different regions, time zones, and customs regulations. But at Sunday, we specialize in handling the complexities of global distribution, so you don't have to.

Whether you need to send bulk deliveries to a trade show or ship personalized gifts to thousands of customers worldwide, our team has the expertise and infrastructure to ensure that your merchandise arrives on time and in perfect condition. We handle everything from customs documentation to address collection, and our system is designed to run campaigns seamlessly once they're set up.

For example, if you're running a global customer appreciation campaign, we can help you design the products, set up the distribution method, and manage the logistics of shipping items to multiple countries. We know what documentation is required for each destination, and we pre-declare items to ensure smooth passage through customs.

7. Technology and innovation at the core

In today's fast-paced business environment, technology is essential for managing complex operations, and merchandise is no exception. That's why we've developed the Sunday Wardrobe platform, a comprehensive dashboard that allows you to manage every aspect of your merchandise operations with ease.

Through Sunday Wardrobe, you can place orders, track shipments, monitor stock levels, and even measure the impact of your merchandise campaigns. Our platform is designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing systems, providing you with a single point of control for all your merchandise needs.

But technology alone isn't enough. We believe in the power of personal service, which is why our team is always available to help you make the most of the tools we provide. Whether you need assistance pulling reports, optimizing campaigns, or planning your next merchandise strategy, our experts are here to support you every step of the way.

8. Collaboration That Drives Results

At Sunday, we believe that the best results come from close collaboration. We don't just see ourselves as your supplier; we see ourselves as your partner in success. This means working together to understand your goals, offering insights and suggestions based on our expertise, and constantly striving to improve and innovate.

Our clients appreciate our proactive approach and our willingness to go the extra mile. We're not just here to take orders; we're here to help you achieve your objectives, whether that's increasing brand awareness, improving employee engagement, or enhancing customer loyalty. By working closely with your team, we ensure that every campaign is not only executed flawlessly but also delivers measurable results.

9. Sustainability with Substance

Sustainability is more than just a trend—it's a commitment to doing business in a way that respects people and the planet. At Sunday, we take sustainability seriously, and it's integrated into every aspect of our operations. You can access our CSR report here.

Our approach to sustainability begins with wearability. We believe that the most sustainable merchandise is the kind that people actually wear and use. That's why we focus on creating high-quality, durable products that people love. If an item is worn frequently, it's not just good for your brand; it's good for the environment because it reduces the need for constant replacement.
