Design inspiration: 20 eye-catching merchandise collections from a range of industries.

Croky: Retro Vibes with a Playful Twist

This vibrant collection from Croky perfectly captures the essence of nostalgia with a modern flair. Featuring bold geometric patterns and the brand's iconic character, these pieces are designed to bring a playful energy to any casual wardrobe. The mix of vibrant colors on soft, high-quality fabrics makes each item not just eye-catching but also incredibly comfortable to wear. Whether you're lounging at home or making a statement on the streets, this collection is all about embracing fun and individuality with every wear.

Napoleon Games: Elegance in Embossed Details

This collection exudes a refined sophistication through its minimalist design, featuring subtle yet impactful embossed portraits on plush, pastel-hued sweatshirts. The luxurious texture of the fabric, combined with the elegant tones of lavender and soft pink, creates a chic and modern aesthetic. Perfect for those who appreciate understated elegance, these pieces offer a versatile option that can transition effortlessly from casual to more polished looks. The intricate embossed detail adds a unique touch, making each piece not just clothing, but a statement of style and confidence.

Human8: On-Brand and Bold: Human8's Rebranding Merchandise

To celebrate and launch their rebranding, Human 8 turned to a collection that's as vibrant and impactful as their new identity. This cohesive set includes a statement sweatshirt, graphic tee, stylish tote bag, cap, and socks, all in their freshly minted brand colors and designs. The mantra “Do what matters” on the sweatshirt encapsulates their core values, while the bold geometric patterns on the other pieces make a powerful visual statement. This collection not only showcases the new brand look but also unifies the team around a shared, refreshed identity, ensuring that every piece is both practical and a proud display of the company's evolution.

Zalando: Celebrating Team Spirit with Stylish Merch

As one of the world's leading online fashion retailers, Zalando knows the importance of making a statement—not just to customers, but also within their team. This collection was designed to celebrate the spirit of teamwork with a perfect blend of style and comfort. Featuring a bold black hoodie with an eye-catching illustration that captures the essence of collaboration, along with a vibrant orange interior for a pop of color, this piece is both functional and fashionable. Complemented by sleek, branded socks and a crisp white tee with subtle detailing, this collection reinforces the message that Zalando's success is built on the strength and unity of their team. Perfect for everyday wear, these items are a testament to the company's commitment to quality and employee appreciation.

Trimetal: Showcasing Professional Pride with Bold Apparel

Trimetal, a leading paint brand for professionals, crafted this striking collection to celebrate and elevate the skilled painters who rely on their products every day. The collection features a sleek black and red color palette, symbolizing strength and precision—qualities synonymous with both the brand and its users. The garments are designed for both style and functionality, making them perfect for on-the-job wear and beyond. From the sharp red detailing on the cap and tees to the bold logo placement, each piece in this collection is a tribute to the craftsmanship and dedication of professional painters. Trimetal's collection not only puts the spotlight on the professionals but also fosters a sense of pride and unity within the industry.

Oaky: Bold Branding Meets High-Impact Fashion

Oaky, a traveltech company known for its vibrant and daring branding, took its identity to the next level with this eye-catching fashion collection. The striking purple hue dominates the design, with oversized, playful typography that makes a statement both on and off the clock. The collection includes a cozy hoodie with contrasting orange drawstrings and a matching tee, both proudly emblazoned with the company's motto, “we're Oaky.” These pieces are designed not just to be worn, but to be seen, turning every employee into a walking ambassador of the brand. With a focus on bold visuals and comfortable wear, this collection perfectly encapsulates Oaky's dynamic and innovative spirit.

Ijsboerke: Serving Up Style with a Sweet Merchandise Collection

Ijsboerke, a beloved name in the world of ice cream, has brought its delightful brand to life with a playful and stylish merchandise collection. This collection captures the fun and nostalgia of enjoying a scoop of your favorite flavor on a warm day. Featuring a bold color palette that echoes the brand's iconic packaging, the collection includes a vibrant red and gray tee emblazoned with the Ijsboerke logo, as well as softer, pastel-hued shirts that subtly incorporate ice cream-themed details. From the classic logo to the whimsical embroidered popsicle, each piece is designed to spread joy and evoke the simple pleasures of summertime treats. This collection is more than just apparel—it's a celebration of Ijsboerke's rich heritage and the happiness it brings to ice cream lovers everywhere.

Johnnie Walker: Celebrating 200 Years with a Bold Streetwear Collection

To mark its 200-year milestone, the iconic whiskey brand Johnnie Walker launched a unique streetwear collection that captures the spirit of its enduring “Keep Walking” mantra. This vibrant collection features bold designs and striking colors, blending the brand's heritage with contemporary fashion. The collection includes statement tees, caps, and accessories, all emblazoned with the “Keep Walking” slogan and the brand's famous striding man logo. From the eye-catching yellow and blue shirts to the sleek branded socks and caps, each piece in the collection not only celebrates the legacy of Johnnie Walker but also makes a powerful statement in today's fashion world. This collection is a testament to the brand's ability to evolve while staying true to its roots, appealing to both whiskey enthusiasts and style-conscious individuals alike.

Next Big Thing: Where Innovation Meets Comfort

The “Next Big Thing” collection seamlessly blends innovation with comfort, offering a premium hoodie and jogger set that's perfect for forward-thinkers on the go. The minimalist design in a soft, neutral beige is accented by bold red details, creating a look that's both sleek and striking. The hoodie proudly features the “Next Big Thing” emblem, signifying a commitment to future-focused thinking. With its comfortable fit and high-quality fabric, this set is ideal for both casual wear and making a subtle, yet powerful, statement about ambition and innovation. Whether you're working from home or brainstorming your next big idea, this collection ensures you do it in style.

Neodyme: High-Tech Inspiration Meets Modern Design

Neodyme's merchandise collection is a perfect reflection of its cutting-edge approach and innovative spirit. The collection features a striking black hoodie with bold graphic lines in neon colors, representing the dynamic and interconnected world of technology. The prominent “Nd” symbol on the hoodie stands out, symbolizing Neodyme's core identity. Complemented by matching accessories like vibrant socks and a sleek laptop sleeve, this collection is designed for the modern professional who values both style and function. The attention to detail—from the contrasting orange drawstrings to the purple-lined hood—makes each piece not just apparel, but a statement of Neodyme's commitment to quality and forward-thinking design.

AVR: Power and Precision in Every Stitch

AVR, a leader in agricultural machinery, has seamlessly woven its brand identity into this collection, emphasizing the power and precision that define their equipment. The deep green hue, synonymous with AVR's branding, takes center stage in this collection, featured prominently on a rugged sweatshirt and playful, logo-emblazoned socks. The word “Machtig” (meaning “Powerful” in Dutch) is boldly embroidered on the sweatshirt, underscoring the strength and reliability that AVR brings to the fields. This collection not only serves as practical workwear but also as a proud display of the company's heritage and commitment to quality. Designed to resonate with those who live and breathe agriculture, these pieces are a testament to AVR's enduring connection with the land and those who work it.

Mercedes: Dual-Function Reversible Jacket with Style and Safety

Mercedes-Benz collaborated on this innovative reversible jacket, merging subtle sophistication with practical safety features. On one side, the jacket presents a sleek, understated look with subtle branding, perfect for everyday wear. However, when turned inside out, the jacket reveals a vibrant, reflective material designed for visibility and safety, especially in low-light conditions. This design ensures that the wearer is not only stylish but also protected. Ideal for those who value both aesthetics and functionality, this jacket is a testament to Mercedes' commitment to quality and innovation, extending their brand ethos into a versatile and thoughtful piece of apparel.

Bequiet: Bold and Fun Merchandise for Every Season

Bequiet, known for its high-performance computing hardware, has extended its brand into the world of merchandise with a collection that's as bold and memorable as their products. From festive Christmas jumpers like the one shown here—featuring playful reindeer and the iconic Bequiet logo—to vibrant Hawaiian shirts and a comprehensive team apparel collection, Bequiet's merchandise is designed to stand out. Each item is crafted with the same attention to detail and quality that Bequiet is known for, making these pieces not just fun to wear but also a true reflection of the brand's innovative spirit and commitment to excellence. Whether it's celebrating the holidays or gearing up for a big event, Bequiet ensures their team and fans are always dressed for the occasion.

Rekkles: The King in the North—Exclusive High-End Merchandise Collection

Renowned professional gamer Rekkles has launched an exclusive, high-end merchandise collection that embodies his legendary status in the esports world. Dubbed “King in the North,” this limited-edition line features premium pieces like this bold hoodie, showcasing a striking red design against a sleek black backdrop. The powerful message, “The King in the North,” is emblazoned across the back, making it clear that this collection is not just apparel, but a statement of dominance and prestige. Crafted with meticulous attention to detail, each item in this collection is designed to resonate with Rekkles' fans and those who appreciate top-tier gaming culture. This is more than just merch—it's a symbol of victory, leadership, and the relentless pursuit of excellence in esports.

Ablo: Bursting with Color in a Vibrant Merchandise Collection

Ablo, the dynamic chat startup, has unveiled a merchandise collection that perfectly mirrors its energetic and playful brand identity. This collection is a celebration of color, featuring bold, popping hues that grab attention and exude positivity. With items like a bright pink and yellow cap, a vibrant green sweatshirt, and a multicolored fanny pack, Ablo's merch is designed to stand out. Each piece proudly displays the Ablo logo, reinforcing brand recognition while radiating fun and approachability. This collection not only appeals to Ablo's youthful and spirited user base but also embodies the company's commitment to making connections more colorful and exciting. Whether you're wearing it to a tech event or just out and about, this merch ensures you'll be noticed.

Accent HR: Launching a Bold New Identity with Pantone-Perfect Merchandise

To celebrate its rebrand, Accent HR unveiled a striking new collection that perfectly matches their refreshed purple and orange color palette. This collection includes a vibrant orange beanie and a rich maroon sweatshirt, each emblazoned with the bold “A” logo, symbolizing the company's renewed identity. Designed with meticulous attention to Pantone color matching, these pieces ensure that every employee is a walking ambassador of the new brand. Whether it's the bright beanie or the eye-catching sweatshirt, Accent HR's new merchandise not only unifies their team but also boldly announces their brand's evolution to the world.

John & Jane: Elevating Team Unity with a Branded Event Apparel Collection

John & Jane, a leading audio and video rental company known for orchestrating large-scale events, has introduced a cohesive merchandise collection designed exclusively for their team. This sleek and professional apparel line, featuring branded hoodies and t-shirts, ensures that every member of the John & Jane team is not only comfortable but also visibly unified at every event. The collection is tailored to withstand the demands of the event industry while maintaining a stylish, modern look that reflects the company's high standards and attention to detail. With this collection, John & Jane reinforces their brand presence while fostering a strong sense of team identity and pride, ensuring that their crew stands out at every event they set up.
