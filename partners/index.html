Agency Partner Program

New revenue stream

We offer agency partners a very transparant commission on our pricing. Sunday invoices directly to the partner and you choose your own pricing towards your client. Upsells like concept, design, strategy & project management are completely up to you.

Partner development

Your designers, project managers and account managers love to learn new things. We engage ourselves to actively train your team to become clothing experts. From partner onboarding to ongoing inspiration sessions, our team commits to educating your team.

Full campaign support

Sunday offers you full support from design assistance to fulfilment through the Sunday Wardrobe. Are your clients looking for clothing for a brand activation campaign or a employee onboarding program? Our team helps you create happy clients.

Quality Apparel for Creative Campaigns

What advantages do I have as an agency?

Offering this extra service to your customer will not only profile you as a full service agency, you will also generate extra revenue. We'll support you in creating company fashion for your client. After all your creative work has been done, we'll do all the operational hassle for you. From technical files to production to delivery. Next to the extra commission fee, samples will be provided during the process.

Why Sunday?

We are not an ordinary embroidery shop. We start from design on a blank page and produce unique clothing. This offers you endless possibilities to translate your creative work onto clothing. Next to design, sustainability, quality and service are very important in our daily business.

I'm interested, what is the next step?

Peaked interest? Please contact our Partner Manager for a brief discovery meeting. We'll explore opportunities and hopefully start a structural partnership when we have a match!
