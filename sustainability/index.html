Our Green Approach

What differentiates Sunday from traditional merch suppliers is that we literally start from the rolls of fabric, and this means we control the full supply chain from design, tech files & patterns to all raw materials and the manufacturing. This is why we can ensure high sustainability standards during the whole process.

Reduce, reuse and recycle

We consider the hierarchy of lowering our environmental impact as “reduce, reuse, recycle”. The greatest strategy is to reduce trash, followed by reusing and recycling products whenever possible.

Reduce
  • Reducing mass production thanks to our mission & vision
  • Reducing waste with technology & quality
  • Reducing water consumption
  • Reduce transport & CO2 emissions due main partners in EU
Reuse
  • Reusing products with campaigns on the Sunday Wardrobe
  • Reusing leftover fabrics to products or dog cushions
  • Reusing water in the production processes
Recycle
  • Recycled plastic pet bottles to make felt products
  • Recycled polyester to make sportswear
  • Recycled fishing nets to yarns for socks
  • Recycled carton for our packaging
