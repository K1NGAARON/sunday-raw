Our Green Approach

We strive to be the best possible sustainable partner for businesses. Thus, we are committed to sustainable materials, - processes and - techniques so that we can continue to improve in these areas and grow as a sustainable player in an often polluting market.

Reduce, reuse and recycle

Sustainable supply chain

Because of the high quality of our materials, you can wear the clothing much longer than promotional merchandise.

By using specific technology we lilimit waste (3D visuals and bulk order pattern list)

We reduce water consumption by using a minimum of it in our production process. Additionally we reduce our carbon footprint by reducing our transportation by producing mainly in Europe.

Our own SaaS platform, The Sunday Wardrobe, makes it possible to ship clothing boxes around the world. We want to give something back to the planet for this and therefore plant a tree for every 50 packages sent through the platform.

Reuse

Because we make custom garments starting from rolls of fabric it sometimes happens that a few meters of fabric are left over. From this, pillows are made for a dog shelter.

Orders placed through the Sunday Wardrobe™ in which not all clothes get used, can simply be placed back into the wardrobe. This way clothing is not thrown away and can be reused.

Recycle

Our clothing is shipped in recycled boxes or biodegradable polybags.

We make sportswear and felt bags made out of recycled pet bottles and recycled polyester.

One of the most popular products are our socks. We wanted a sustainable version of it and now work with Econyl, a fabric made out of recycled fishing nets.
