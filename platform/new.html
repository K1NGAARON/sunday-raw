What is it?

It's a platform that makes it super easy for companies to manage & scale their
merchandise in a time and cost saving way.

This is done on three levels:

1

Level

Unlimited merchandise design & order platform

Sunday designs all your merchandise perfectly on brand.

All designs are shown on your order platform with tier pricing.

Consistent branding across all departments & use cases.

2

Level

Simplified order shop

The webshop is user friendly and makes it possible to present products in a nice and professional way.

Collecting orders and addresses is supereasy.

Reward ambassadors through a coin/point system on the webshop.

3

Level

Warehousing & shipping

We store your merchandise safely at our warehouse.

We pick and package your merchandise boxes.

We ship your merchandise anywhere in the world. (even handling customs).
