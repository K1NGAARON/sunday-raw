Local Heroes of Brooklyn

The design brief

With the 'Local Heroes' collection, Brooklyn wanted to express their love for the 7 cities in which they have a store, with a touch of humor, and emphasize the importance of 'shopping local'!

Sunday designed a collection of 100% sustainable T-shirts that refer to well-known attractions and symbols in a humorous way: Kortrijk, Roeselare, Ostend, Bruges, Knokke, Ghent and Waregem. Our detailed embroidery ensures that the collection has a premium quality look.
