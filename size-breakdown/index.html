Easy sizing tool for your company fashion

Let’s face it: “What sizes should we get?” is one of the most common questions we get from our customers.
We made a handy calculator you can use for choosing the exact size breakdown for your group order. Regardless, if there are any questions or doubts our experts are still here to assist you.

Size breakdown calculator

This calculator applies our recommended ratio of unisex sizes to determine how many of each size you need to order, based on your total number of items. It even rounds up and down for you. Just enter your total number of wearables.

Recommendation of size distribution

To prepare the calculator, we looked at survey data, our own customer data, and discussed the topic with our sales managers to come up with the size distribution breakdown that can work for any large order of clothing items.

Our recommendation takes the numbers per size of unisex items purchased in the EU– which matches exactly with our internal sales data– then skews the numbers slightly towards the larger sizes to be more correct to the true percentage per size. In total, it breaks down like this:

XS ≈ 0%

M ≈ 29.9%

XL ≈ 17.1%

S ≈ 17.1%

L ≈ 29.9%

2XL ≈ 6.1%

Get in touch with on of our experts

We hope this guide makes it easier for you to order the right sizes of your company fashion items. Yet, this article is based on buying unisex items for everyone. Depending on your market or end-user, you may want to split the order between men’s and women’s styles.

Yes, it is still possible to consult with our sales experts about a different sizing of your items. The more info we have on the purpose of your company fashion collection and the corresponding target group of the company, the more we can offer a tailor-made proposal.
